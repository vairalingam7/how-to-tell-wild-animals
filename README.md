<h3>🐅 Big Cats</h3>
<ul>
  <li>
    <strong>🦁 Lion</strong> → Known as the "King of the Jungle"; roars can be heard up to 8 km away; lives in prides; symbolizes strength and courage.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>🐯 Tiger</strong> → Largest of the big cats; orange coat with black stripes; powerful hunter; solitary by nature; excellent swimmer.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>🐆 Leopard</strong> → Agile climber; golden coat with dark rosettes; stealthy predator; drags prey into trees; adaptable to many habitats.
  </li>
</ul>

<h3>🐊 Fierce Friends</h3>
<ul>
  <li>
    <strong>🐻 Bear</strong> → Strong and heavy; can stand upright; known for a crushing hug; omnivorous diet; hibernates during winter.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>🐊 Crocodile</strong> → Semi-aquatic reptile; powerful jaws; sheds "crocodile tears" while eating; ambush predator; ancient lineage dating back millions of years.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>🦉 Hyena</strong> → Recognizable by its eerie "laugh"; scavenger but also skilled hunter; strong jaws for crushing bones; lives in clans; plays a key role in ecosystems.
  </li>
</ul>

<h3>🦎 Oddballs</h3>
<ul>
  <li>
    <strong>🦎 Chameleon</strong> → Small lizard; changes skin color to blend with surroundings; independently moving eyes; long sticky tongue; symbolizes adaptability and camouflage.
  </li>
</ul>
